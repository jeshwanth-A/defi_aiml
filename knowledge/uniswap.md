# Uniswap Overview

Uniswap is a decentralized exchange protocol built around automated market making. It is widely associated with permissionless token trading and on-chain liquidity provisioning.

## Common Uses

- token swaps through liquidity pools
- price discovery for long-tail assets
- on-chain liquidity infrastructure used by other apps

## Key Risks

- liquidity fragmentation across chains or venues
- smart contract or governance risk
- fee pressure from competing DEX designs
- market share shifts during changes in trader behavior

## Project Notes

When the assistant explains Uniswap, it should distinguish the protocol from the UNI token. Protocol adoption does not always translate directly into token price strength on short horizons.
