# Ethereum Overview

Ethereum is a programmable blockchain focused on smart contracts and decentralized applications. It is central to many DeFi, NFT, and infrastructure ecosystems.

## Common Uses

- smart contract execution
- DeFi lending, trading, and derivatives
- infrastructure for token issuance and on-chain applications

## Key Risks

- fee spikes during periods of high network activity
- competition from faster or lower-cost chains
- smart contract and protocol-level risks in the surrounding ecosystem

## Project Notes

In DefiDoza, Ethereum questions often mix fundamentals with market behavior. Good answers should separate network utility, ecosystem strength, and the distinct risks of ETH price forecasting.
