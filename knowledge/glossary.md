# Crypto Glossary

## RAG

Retrieval-augmented generation is a pattern where the assistant looks up relevant documents at question time and uses that retrieved text as context for the final answer.

## Forecast

A forecast is a model-generated estimate about a future value. In this project, forecasts are outputs from locally trained models and should not be treated as guarantees.

## Volatility

Volatility describes how sharply price moves over time. Higher volatility usually means wider and faster swings in either direction.

## Momentum

Momentum is a measure of how strongly price has been moving in a recent direction. Positive momentum suggests recent upward movement, while negative momentum suggests recent weakness.

## Liquidity

Liquidity describes how easily an asset can be bought or sold without causing large price movement. Deep liquidity often reduces execution friction but does not remove market risk.
