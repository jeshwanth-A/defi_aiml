# Bitcoin Overview

Bitcoin is the original cryptocurrency and is commonly treated as the market reference asset for the broader crypto ecosystem. Its main value propositions are scarcity, security, and liquidity.

## Common Uses

- store of value narratives
- treasury reserve experimentation
- collateral or benchmark asset in trading discussions

## Key Risks

- high volatility during macro stress or liquidity shocks
- regulatory changes affecting custody, access, or exchange activity
- narrative-driven price swings that can decouple from short-term fundamentals

## Project Notes

Within DefiDoza, Bitcoin should usually be framed as the most established supported asset, but not as a low-risk asset. When summarizing outlook, distinguish between relative maturity and absolute price safety.
