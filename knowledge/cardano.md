# Cardano Overview

Cardano is a proof-of-stake blockchain positioned around research-driven development and layered protocol design. It is often described as methodical and governance-focused relative to faster-moving ecosystems.

## Common Uses

- staking and delegation
- smart contract experimentation
- governance-oriented community participation

## Key Risks

- slower ecosystem expansion than higher-velocity competitors
- adoption risk if developer or user growth lags
- price underperformance during periods that reward faster narratives

## Project Notes

Within DefiDoza, Cardano should be described as a supported asset with a strong community identity but a different pace of ecosystem growth than Ethereum or Solana.
